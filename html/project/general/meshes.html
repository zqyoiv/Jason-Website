MESHES

Mesh. Hand-soldered one light at a time.

After fabrication of the LED mesh, the resulting topography of lights is suspended in space from an electrified structure. The material lends itself to smooth rolling forms devoid of sharp folds or creases. Each piece is custom made and shaped.

Krugman has been working with this material for over 10 years. ETL listed and dimmable.
