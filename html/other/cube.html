Cube

In 2011, Jason Krugman Studio worked with Wired Magazine, MKG and James Bedell to produce several custom chandeliers for Wired's Times Square Pop-Up Store. We spent several week designing and fabricating the chandeliers. Made with ten's of thousands of cool white LEDs mounted on steel frames, the chandeliers were up to 4' cubed, but still appeared to be light and airy because of their large amount of open area.
