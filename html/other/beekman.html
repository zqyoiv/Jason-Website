BEEKMAN - CLOUD FORMATIONS

For the Beekman Hotel's grand re-opening in September 2016, Krugman Studio created two large, outdoor sculptures. The artworks' shapes were inspired by Hans Haacke's Blue Sail artwork featuring a piece of blue fabric being blown by a single fan. The resulting simplicity is stunning and inspired Krugman to create variations on a 3-dimensional sin wave expressed through his new mesh medium.

Art Consultant: Art Bridge
